About Our Practice

A Small Practice With Big Expertise

Treasure Valley Foot and Ankle is a small, family-owned podiatry practice dedicated to providing exceptional foot and ankle care. Our top priority is excellent patient care, combining Dr. Johnson's education, training, and experience with genuine care and concern for each patient.

When you visit our clinic, you will feel comfortable and informed each step of the way, from diagnosis through treatment and beyond. We treat everything from minor sprains to severe breaks, including bunions, fungal conditions, and complex surgical cases.

Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Clark Johnson

Board Certified Podiatric Surgeon

Dr. Clark Johnson was born and raised on a cattle ranch in Durango, Colorado. He completed his undergraduate studies at Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah, where he developed his passion for medicine and patient care.

Dr. Johnson received his Doctor of Podiatric Medicine degree at Des Moines University in Des Moines, Iowa, where he also earned a Master of Science degree in Anatomy. He completed his specialized foot and ankle surgery training at the North Colorado Podiatric Medicine and Surgery residency.

As a board certified podiatrist with dual certification from the American Board of Foot and Ankle Surgery for both Foot Surgery and Reconstructive Rearfoot/Ankle Surgery, Dr. Johnson provides comprehensive foot and ankle care, combining his extensive education and training with genuine care and concern for each patient. He speaks both English and Romanian.

Outside of work, Dr. Johnson enjoys outdoor activities including hiking, horseback riding, fishing, snowboarding, and boating with his family.
