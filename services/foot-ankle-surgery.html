Foot & Ankle Surgery

When surgery is the best path forward, you're in expert hands. Dr. Johnson combines advanced techniques with compassionate care for optimal outcomes.

Board-Certified Surgical Expertise

Dr. Johnson holds dual board certification from the American Board of Foot and Ankle Surgery in both Foot Surgery and Reconstructive Rearfoot/Ankle Surgery. This advanced certification means he has demonstrated expertise in the most complex foot and ankle procedures.

Surgery is never our first recommendation - we always explore conservative options first. But when surgery is the best solution, you can feel confident knowing you're receiving care from a highly trained specialist who performs these procedures regularly.

Minimally Invasive Techniques

Whenever possible, Dr. Johnson uses minimally invasive surgical techniques. These approaches offer several advantages:

  • Smaller incisions and less scarring
  • Reduced pain after surgery
  • Faster recovery times
  • Lower risk of complications
  • Often performed as outpatient procedures

What to Expect

Before Surgery

We'll thoroughly explain your procedure, answer all your questions, and ensure you're fully prepared. This includes pre-operative instructions, what to bring, and what to expect during recovery.

Day of Surgery

Most procedures are performed at Millennium Surgery Center or one of our affiliated hospitals. Many surgeries are outpatient, meaning you go home the same day.

After Surgery

We provide detailed post-operative instructions and schedule follow-up appointments to monitor your healing. Our team is always available to answer questions and address concerns during your recovery.
